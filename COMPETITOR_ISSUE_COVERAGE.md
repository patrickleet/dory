# Competitor issue coverage

Last refreshed from the live GitHub issue trackers on 2026-07-13.

This is a release-risk checklist, not marketing copy. The Apple Silicon launch has no partial
support tier: an advertised capability is either **FULL** and protected by a mandatory release
gate, or it is a **LAUNCH BLOCKER** and cannot be marketed or shipped as complete. A blocker may be
implemented but awaiting exact-artifact/environment certification, or may still require product
work; the closure column says which. Intel-host support is a later product track and is not counted
as Apple Silicon incompleteness. The common-amd64-image contract uses Dory's bundled FEX runtime;
untested x86-only products remain outside that advertised contract.
